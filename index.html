About

Jack was born in France and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He believes in the power of art to awaken our imagination to new places, far-off worlds, the inexhaustible wealth of stories and experience.

His work explores themes of memory, displacement, and the intersection of cultures through a variety of mediums including oil painting, charcoal, watercolor, and mixed media.
